Process

How It Works

Four simple steps that run completely on autopilot every single day.

1
Keyword Search Across YouTube
Each day at 2PM, the tracker fires off searches for 10 curated AI/automation keywords — "AI tools 2025", "AI agents explained", "n8n automation workflow", and more. Every search returns the freshest videos posted in the last 24 hours.
2
Deduplicate & Fetch Statistics
All video IDs are merged and deduplicated — a video appearing in multiple keyword results is only analyzed once. Then full statistics (views, likes, comments) are fetched efficiently across all results.
3
Analyze Outperformance
Each video is scored against three criteria: 20K+ views (viral signal), 5K+ views + 5%+ engagement (quality signal), or exceptional engagement rate alone. Videos meeting any criteria are flagged with a reason.
4
Send Beautiful Email Report
A premium HTML email is composed with all outperforming videos sorted by views — showing thumbnail, channel, title, stats, and why each video was flagged. Delivered to your inbox in seconds.
